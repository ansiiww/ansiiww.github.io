About Ingrid

I am a computer science student at Harvey Mudd College grauating in 2023 Spring. My research area is human-robot interaction, and my specific interests include robot ethics, natural language generation, robot gender presentation, and mental models of robot identity.

As an undergrad student, I enjoy the opportunity to grade and tutor for several classes at Harvey Mudd, including Principles and Practice of Computer Science, Algorithms, Natural Language Processing and Modern Physics Lab. When I'm not working, I'm dancing or climbing rocks.
